FAQs

How can I update my personal information?

To update your personal information, navigate to the "My Profile" page and fill out the relevant fields with the updated information. Then, click on the "Save Changes" button.

Can I change my notification preferences?

Yes, you can change your notification preferences by toggling the "Allow notification messages to this phone number" checkbox on the "My Profile" page.

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, click on the "Change Password" link on the "My Profile" page. You will be redirected to a page where you can enter a new password.

What should I do if I encounter technical issues?

If you encounter any technical issues while using the platform, please contact our support team for assistance. You can find contact information on the "Info Desk" page.
